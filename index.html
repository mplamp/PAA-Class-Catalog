(Studio Discovery)

A mini-musical based on Aesop's fable "the Lion and the Mouse" for Unison and 2-part Vocies By Andy Beck and Brain Fisher

In this class, there is a fun, game-filled exploration of acting fundamentals, stage directions, and character development. The children apply their skills in hands-on learning through the making of a mini-musical, complete with lines, singing and dancing!In addition to discovering and artistically using the items in the show trunk, the students will also create crafts of their own in support of the musical. These may include props, costume-related pieces such as masks, and environmental support, such as drawings for scenery.

On the final day of class, we invite the young peoples' family and friends to join us for a sharing of the musical in a supportive classroom setting, complete with story-necessary props and costume pieces. The sharing will be kept simple, with the focus on the students and their creativity.

In addition, the students will participate in the all-class Shareformance at the end of the Fall classes, in which they will perform an ensemble number from the musical!

Voice Classes

(Studio Discovery)

Music is a powerful and playful tool. Learn how to play instruments and sing, creating music with your friends! This class will be more fun than you can shake a shaker at!

(Studio 3)

In Voice class, students work on developing the vocabulary of music as it is used in the art and practice of musical theatre. From foundational principles, to using music for storytelling and character journey, the emphasis is on practical application of music on the stage as a part of a larger theatrical narrative. Practice may include short solos similar to a monologue in music form, duets, and ensemble pieces. Homework of music review and preparation may be asked.
